About VIP

Viosimos Integrated Planning Consultant’s Inc. provides consultancy services to both the private and the public sector. The company provides assistance in the development of Ecological Profiles (EP), Comprehensive Land Use Plans (CLUP), Disaster Risk Reduction and Climate Change Action Plans (DRR/CCA), Comprehensive Development Plans (CDP), Project Feasibility Studies, transportation and infrastructure plans, and Engineering Geological and Geohazard Assessment (EGGAR). We also engage in Geographic Information System Mapping or Tax Mapping as well as other various areas of training and research and development studies.

Viósimos is a Greek work which means “sustainable.” It completely ties in with our primary mission, which is to uphold environmental protection and the sustainable development and management of resources through proper stakeholder engagement. We aim to accomplish this through rational planning that produces practical and sustainable solutions for the spatial planning challenges caused by urban development. Our goal is to also take part in projects that bring about major improvements in society, particularly with the living conditions of ordinary citizens.
