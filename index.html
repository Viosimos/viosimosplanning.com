About VIP

VIP stands for Viosimos Integrated Planning. Viósimos is a Greek word meaning sustainable, a quality that is at the core of our values as an organization. VIP Consultants, Inc. is an organization with the purpose to promote sustainable development and to protect the general welfare by conducting basic and rational applied research and consultancy services in various fields of environmental, urban, and regional planning.

The organization provides consultancy services to both public and private sectors, particularly in key areas such as Ecological Profiling (EP), Comprehensive Land Use Planning (CLUP), Disaster Risk Reduction and Climate Change Action Planning (DRR/CCA), Comprehensive Development Planning (CDP), Project Feasibility Studies, Transportation and Infrastructure Planning, Engineering Geological and Geohazard Assessment (EGGAR), Geographic Information System Mapping/ Tax Mapping, and engages in various other areas of training, research and development studies.

Overall, our organization’s goal is to provide technical assistance in the production of rational planning documents, incorporating practical and sustainable solutions for spatial planning challenges as a driver of urban development to improve living conditions as we progress through development in the society.
